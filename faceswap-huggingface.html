🎯 Best Results
Use clear, front-facing photos with good lighting. Both images should have fully visible faces. Avoid sunglasses, extreme angles, or heavy filters.
⚙️ How It Works
Uses Hugging Face Gradio Spaces as a free API. Your images are sent directly to the HF server, face-swapped by AI, then returned to your browser.
🐢 Still Slow?
HF Spaces go to "sleep" when unused (cold start). Use Auto mode for parallel requests, or wait 30s and retry. Subsequent swaps are much faster.
